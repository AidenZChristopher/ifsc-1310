Frequently Asked Questions

When was LEGO founded?

LEGO was founded on August 10, 1932.

Where was the first LEGO brick produced?

The first LEGO brick was produced in Billund, Denmark, in 1949.

Who currently owns and manages LEGO?

LEGO is privately owned by KIRKBI A/S, a holding company controlled by the Kristiansen family. Niels B. Christiansen is the current CEO (as of 2024).

How has each generation of the Kristiansen family influenced the company's growth?
  • Ole Kirk Christiansen (Founder): Established the company and created wooden toys.
  • Godtfred Kirk Christiansen (2nd Generation): Developed the LEGO System of Play and transitioned the brand into plastic bricks.
  • Kjeld Kirk Kristiansen (3rd Generation): Expanded the brand globally, initiated licensing deals, and established LEGOLAND theme parks.
  • Thomas Kirk Kristiansen (4th Generation): Currently chairs LEGO’s board, emphasizing sustainability, digital innovation, and expanding the LEGO brand's global footprint.
When were plastic LEGO bricks first introduced?

Plastic LEGO bricks were first introduced in 1949.

When did LEGO begin producing themed sets, such as LEGO Star Wars or LEGO City?

LEGO launched its first licensed theme, LEGO Star Wars, in 1999. LEGO City’s predecessor, LEGO Town, began earlier in 1978.
